TREASURY UPDATE



Our initial plan was to invest in HORDE and Grape finance. Due to the fact that we're whitelisted for SolarProtocol and able to buy at a discount, we've decided that it would be better to sit on our hands until pre-sale launch, allowing us to make as many nodes as possible. For those that asked, we'll still be diversifying into grape and horde once we start accruing rewards from Solar "nodes".

$STRNGR is still in a downtrend and we won't be investing anymore into Stronger nodes until we receive confirmation or further updates regarding StrongChain. We're still bullish long term and looking forward to Dapps being built on top of StrongChain, driving demand for Stronger. Until then we'll act accordingly so we can continue with giveaways, if holders choose it.
